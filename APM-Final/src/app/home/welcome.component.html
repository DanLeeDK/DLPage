Pretty pets is a pet agency, who collects all cute and beautiful pets and gives them a chance make some earnings by participating in commercials, photoshoot, events and more.

Create an account for you and your pet. Take some good pictures that shows the looks and talents of your pet. Companies in search for a pet like yours will then contact you and give you an offer for a job or you can preset your asking price.

It is up to you to set an asking price or accept what a company is willing to offer you. Normally it's a good idea to start low and see how the demand for you pet is.
